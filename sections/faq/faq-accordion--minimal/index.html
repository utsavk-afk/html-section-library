Questions

Everything you'd ask before signing up.

The short version of the things support gets asked most. Still stuck on something specific?

Email hello@parcelbase.io — we reply within a day.

01 Can I move my data over from another tool?

Yes. Import CSV exports from Airtable, Notion, or a plain spreadsheet and we map columns automatically. Anything we can't match gets flagged so you can fix it before the records go live.

Larger migrations (over 50k rows) are handled by our team at no cost — reply to your welcome email and we'll schedule it.

02 How does pricing work as my team grows?

You're billed per active editor, per month. Viewers and commenters are always free, so you only pay for people who actually build and change things.

Downgrade or remove seats anytime and the change is prorated on your next invoice.

03 Is there a limit on records or storage?

The Standard plan covers up to 250,000 records and 25 GB of attachments per workspace. Teams that outgrow that move to Scale, which lifts both limits and adds audit logs.

04 Do you have an API and webhooks?

Every table gets a REST endpoint the moment you create it. Authenticate with a scoped token, then read and write with the same field names you see in the app.

Outbound webhooks fire on record.created, record.updated, and record.deleted, with retries for 24 hours.

05 What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your workspace stays read-only for 30 days after cancellation so you can export at your own pace. Nothing is deleted before that window closes.

Request a full account wipe sooner and we process it within 72 hours.

06 Which regions do you host in?

Data is stored in either the EU (Frankfurt) or US (Virginia) — you choose at signup, and it never leaves that region. Backups are encrypted and kept in the same location.
