Who we are

We build tools makers actually keep.

Blockpaste started in 2021 with a stubborn idea: the best components shouldn't live behind a paywall or rot inside a private repo. So we shipped the first fifty for free — and never stopped.

Today it's a library of 420+ copy-paste sections, hand-built by a team of six who still read every bug report and answer their own support tickets. No VC, no roadmap theater. Just code that pastes clean and ships the same day.
