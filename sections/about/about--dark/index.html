Our story · est. 2016

We build tools for people who’d rather ship than configure.

Sundry started as a shared frustration: three engineers tired of wiring the same auth, billing, and deploy plumbing into every side project. So we spent a weekend collapsing it into one command — and never went back.

Ten years on, that instinct still runs the company. We keep the surface area small, the defaults honest, and the docs written by the same folks who wrote the code. No growth team, no dark patterns — just software we’d want on our own machines.
