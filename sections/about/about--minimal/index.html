Our story

We started Fieldnote because good research kept dying in slide decks nobody reopened. So we built a studio around one stubborn idea: the work is only finished when a customer feels it.

In 2019 we were three people sharing one desk in Porto, redrawing a checkout flow for the fourth time. The client shipped it. Sales moved. That loop — draw, ship, watch, redraw — became the whole company.

We stayed small on purpose. No account layers, no handoff theater. The person who sketches your interface is the person who answers your Thursday email and sits in your launch call.
