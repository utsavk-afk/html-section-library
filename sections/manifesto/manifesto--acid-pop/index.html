The House Rules v3.0 / no apologies Read it twice

What we actually believe

Good enough is a slow leak. We ship things that refuse to be ignored.

  1. 01

    Boring is the only real risk. A safe design nobody remembers costs more than a loud one that gets argued about. We take the argument.

  2. 02

    Taste is a decision, not a mood. Every color, every gap, every hard edge is chosen on purpose and defended out loud. Nothing is here by accident.

  3. 03

    Ship it before it's precious. Perfect is a hiding place. We put work in front of real people while we still have the nerve to change it.

  4. 04

    Copy that whispers gets deleted. If a headline could belong to any brand on earth, it belongs to none. We write the sentence only we would say.

Signed, everyone who refused to round the corners. — The whole studio.
