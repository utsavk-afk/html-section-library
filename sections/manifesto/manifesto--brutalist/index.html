// The House Rules Ships fast shop  ·  Draft 07 / No apologies

We don't pivot.
We build the thing.

Seven beliefs we refuse to negotiate. Print them. Tape them to the wall. If a decision breaks one of these, the decision is wrong — not the rule.

01

Ship it Friday

A rough thing in real hands beats a perfect thing in a slide deck. Deadlines are features. Momentum is a moat.

02

Opinions have owners

Every call gets a name attached. No committees, no "we decided." If you can't sign it, don't say it.

03

Boring tech, loud results

We pick the tool that's still here in five years, not the one trending this week. Novelty is a tax we don't pay.

04

Cut the meeting

If it could've been a paragraph, it should've been. Async by default. Time is the only thing we can't refund.

05

Say the hard thing

Kind is not the same as nice. We give the note early, in plain words, to your face — never behind your back.

06

Own the mess

You broke it, you name it, you fix it in daylight. Blame is expensive. A clean post-mortem is free.

07

Delight is non-optional

The last 5% is the whole product. Polish the corners nobody asked about. Someone always notices.

✕

And one rule to break them

When a rule stops serving the work, rewrite it out loud. Dogma is just a habit that forgot its reason.
