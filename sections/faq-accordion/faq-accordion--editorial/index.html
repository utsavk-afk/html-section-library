01 How long before we see measurable results?

Technical fixes and on-page improvements often show movement within 4 to 6 weeks. Meaningful organic traffic gains typically land in the 3 to 5 month window, depending on your domain history and competition.

We share a baseline report on day one so every claim after that is measured against real numbers — not vanity dashboards.

02 Do you require a long-term contract?

No. We work on a rolling monthly retainer with a 30-day notice period. We ask for a soft three-month runway only because SEO compounds — leaving after four weeks rarely does the work justice.

If we're not the right fit, we'll tell you before you sign.

03 Who actually does the work on my account?

A named strategist owns your account end to end — the same person you meet in the kickoff call. They're supported by our in-house content and link teams, but you're never handed off to a rotating queue.

No offshore black boxes, no ticket-number relationships.

04 How is pricing structured?

Flat monthly retainers scoped to the work — not to your revenue. Plans start at $2,400/mo for focused campaigns and scale with content volume, technical depth, and link targets.

Every proposal itemizes exactly what's included. You'll never get a surprise line item.

05 Can you work alongside our existing marketing team?

Absolutely — roughly half our clients have an in-house team. We plug into your Slack, your project board, and your content calendar, acting as the specialist layer rather than a replacement.

We document everything so your team is never dependent on us to keep moving.

06 What reporting do we get, and how often?

A live dashboard you can open any day, plus a monthly written note where a human explains what moved, what didn't, and what we're changing next. We report on rankings, qualified traffic, and pipeline — the metrics tied to money.

07 Do you guarantee first-page rankings?

No honest agency can, and anyone who does is selling you a story. What we do guarantee is a transparent process, defensible tactics that survive algorithm updates, and a clear line from our work to your results.

We'd rather keep your trust than promise a position we don't control.
