The stuff sales pages skip: refunds, migration, seat limits, and who actually owns your data. Straight answers, no asterisks.

Still stuck? Ping a human →
01 How fast can I move my current project over?
Most teams are fully switched in under a weekend. Our importer pulls boards, tasks, comments, and attachments straight from Trello, Asana, Notion, and Linear — nothing is retyped by hand. A migration engineer double-checks the mapping before you flip the switch. See the import checklist.
02 Is there a free plan, or just a trial?
Both. The Free tier is genuinely free forever — up to 3 workspaces and 5 collaborators, no card required. The 14-day Pro trial unlocks automations, guest access, and reporting so you can test the parts that actually cost money before you commit to them.
03 What happens to my data if I cancel?
You own it, full stop. Export everything to JSON or CSV in one click, any time — before, during, or after cancellation. We keep a read-only copy for 30 days in case you change your mind, then it's permanently wiped from our servers and backups.
04 Can I add and remove seats mid-cycle?
Yes — add someone at 2pm on a Tuesday and you're billed a prorated amount only for the days they're active. Remove a seat and the credit rolls straight onto your next invoice. No annual lock-in, no "contact sales to downgrade" nonsense.
05 Do you actually offer real human support?
Every plan — including Free — gets live chat with a real person, Mon–Fri, 7am to 8pm CET. Median first reply is 8 minutes. Pro and Business plans add a private Slack channel and a named onboarding lead for your first month.
06 How do you handle security and compliance?
We're SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR-compliant, with data encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). SSO/SAML, SCIM provisioning, and audit logs ship on the Business plan. Read the full trust report.
07 Will it connect to the tools we already use?
Out of the box it talks to Slack, GitHub, Figma, Google Drive, and Zapier, plus a documented REST API and webhooks for everything else. Building a custom integration? Our developer sandbox is open on every plan, trial included.
