Updated 22 Jul 2026 · 7 entries

01 How fast do I get onboarded?
Same business day. You send the kickoff form, we spin up your workspace and drop the access keys within four hours. First deliverable lands inside 72 hours — no "discovery phase" that quietly eats three weeks of your retainer.
02 Is there a lock-in contract?
Nope. Everything runs month-to-month. Cancel before the 25th and you won't be billed for the next cycle. You keep every file, brief, and export we ever handed you — no hostage clauses, no exit fees.
03 Who actually owns the work?
You do. Full commercial rights transfer to your entity the moment an invoice clears. Source files, editable layers, the works. We keep a copy for support only and never resell or repurpose it for another client.
04 Can you plug into our stack?
Yes — Slack, Notion, Linear, GitHub, HubSpot and a dozen more via native connectors. If it has a webhook or an API, we can wire it in. Custom integrations get scoped on the Scale plan at no extra hourly rate.
05 What if I hate the first draft?
You get unlimited revisions inside the sprint window — no per-round charges, no passive-aggressive "scope creep" emails. If round three still misses, we reassign the lead and re-run it free. Roughly 2% of projects ever need that.
06 How does billing work?
Flat monthly fee, charged on the 1st, invoiced not metered. No surprise overages, no per-seat math. Card, ACH, or wire — your call. Annual prepay knocks 18% off and locks your rate for the full year.
07 Is my data kept private?
Encrypted at rest and in transit, SOC 2 Type II audited, EU and US regions available. We don't train models on your content and we don't share it with third parties. Full sub-processor list is public — read the security brief.
