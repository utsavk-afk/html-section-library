01 How long does migrating an existing project actually take?

Most teams are running in production within a single afternoon. Our CLI reads your current config, generates the Halden equivalent, and flags anything it can’t map automatically so you review it once instead of debugging it later. A monorepo with a dozen services typically lands in under two hours.

02 What happens to my data if I decide to cancel?

You keep it. Export the full dataset as newline-delimited JSON or Parquet at any time, no support ticket required. After cancellation we retain a cold copy for 30 days in case you change your mind, then it’s permanently purged and we send you a signed deletion receipt.

03 Do you charge per seat, or by usage?

By usage — measured in compute-seconds and egress, billed to the cent. Invite your whole team, contractors and all, at no extra cost. We’d rather you add reviewers than share one login. See the pricing breakdown for the exact per-unit rates.

04 Is there a hard limit on requests during a traffic spike?

No throttling ceiling on paid plans. Capacity scales automatically and we alert you the moment a spike pushes you into a new billing tier, so a viral launch never gets silently rate-limited. If you’re expecting a known event, tell us and we’ll pre-warm the region.

05 Which regions and compliance standards do you cover?

Eleven regions across North America, the EU, and APAC, with data residency pinning so records never leave the jurisdiction you choose. We’re SOC 2 Type II and ISO 27001 certified; HIPAA and GDPR data-processing addenda are available on request for Business and Enterprise accounts.

06 Can I try it before putting a card on file?

Yes. The developer tier is free forever — no card, no trial countdown, no feature crippling. It covers roughly 50,000 requests a month, which is enough to ship a small side project and never pay a cent. You only reach for a card when your traffic genuinely outgrows it.

07 How do you handle incidents at 3 a.m.?

A human on-call engineer, not a chatbot, is paged for every Sev-1. Our public status page updates in real time, and Business accounts get a direct Slack channel with our platform team. Post-incident we publish a full write-up within five business days — root cause included, no marketing gloss.
