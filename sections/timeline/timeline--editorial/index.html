The Long Way Here

A workshop that grew up one honest revision at a time.

No overnight story. Just five moments where the shop changed shape, told in the order they actually happened.

2011

Two benches, one back room

Started binding notebooks for friends after hours, using leather offcuts from a shoemaker down the street.

The beginning
2015

First storefront on Ash Lane

Traded the back room for a corner shop with real windows. Sold out the opening run in nine days.

Signed the lease
2018

The refillable ledger

Redesigned every cover to be re-stitched, not replaced. Repairs quietly became a third of the business.

Our turning point
2022

Apprentices, not hires

Opened a two-year bench program. Four makers trained; three still cut leather beside us today.

Passing it on
Now — 2026

Bound in eleven countries

Same benches, same stitch. The only thing that scaled was the number of hands we ship to.

Where we are

Every date above was a decision we could have skipped — and didn't.
