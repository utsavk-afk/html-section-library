Do we own the code, or are we renting it from you?

You own 100% of it. Everything ships to your GitHub org on day one, MIT-licensed on our side, no runtime tied to our servers. If you ever fire us, the build keeps running and any dev can pick it up — no vendor lock, no “platform fee” quietly renewing in the background.

How fast can we actually go live?

A standard marketing site lands in 9–14 business days. Anything with auth, billing, or a dashboard runs 4–6 weeks. We give you a dated milestone plan in the kickoff call, not a vague “soon.”

02

What happens if a deadline slips?

If the delay is on us, that week is free — automatically credited, no argument required. We’d rather eat the cost than defend a missed date.

03

Can you work inside our existing stack?

Yes — Next.js, Rails, Django, Laravel, plain HTML. We adapt to your repo and conventions instead of forcing a rewrite. We’ll flag it early if something is genuinely holding you back.

04

Who do we talk to day-to-day?

The engineer building your project — not an account manager relaying messages. One shared Slack channel, replies during your working hours, no ticket queue.
