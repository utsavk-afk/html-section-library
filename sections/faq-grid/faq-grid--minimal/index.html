01

How long does migrating from Notion or Airtable actually take?

Most teams under 40 seats are fully moved in a single afternoon. Our importer maps your existing fields, keeps comment history intact, and runs a dry-run first so nothing overwrites live data.

02

What counts as a “seat” on a paid plan?

Anyone who creates or edits. Viewers, clients, and guests you share a board with are always free — you’re only billed for the people doing the building.

03

Is my data used to train any AI models?

No. Customer content is never used for training, and the assistant runs on an isolated tenant. You can disable AI features entirely from the admin panel with one toggle.

04

Can I export everything if we decide to leave?

Yes, no lock-in. Export any workspace to Markdown, CSV, or JSON at any time, or pull it live through the public API. Deleted accounts are purged within 30 days.

05

Do you offer a discount for nonprofits or students?

Registered nonprofits get 50% off any tier, and verified students get the Pro plan free for a year. Send proof to billing and we’ll apply it the same day.

06

What happens when I hit my plan’s limits mid-month?

Nothing breaks. You keep read and write access; we just email an admin and pause new seat invites until you upgrade. No surprise overage charges, ever.

07

Which SSO and compliance standards do you support?

SAML and SCIM ship on the Business plan, with Okta, Google, and Entra tested. We’re SOC 2 Type II certified — the report is available under NDA.

08

Can I try it before putting in a card?

The 14-day trial needs no card and includes every Pro feature. If you need longer to get buy-in, ask and we’ll extend it — that’s a normal request, not a favor.
