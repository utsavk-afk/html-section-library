Before you ask

Questions we hear most weeks

The honest answers we give on discovery calls — pricing, timelines, and what actually happens after you sign. Still curious? Write to us directly.

01

How long until we see the first draft?

Ten business days from kickoff. You get a full first pass — not a mood board — so there's something real to react to. Two rounds of revision are built into every engagement.

02

Do you work with our existing brand guidelines?

Always. We treat your guidelines as the constraint, not the enemy. If something in them is quietly hurting conversion, we'll flag it — with evidence — and let you decide.

03

Who owns the work when we're done?

You do — files, fonts, and full source. On final invoice we hand over an editable package and a short walkthrough video. No license leash, no vendor lock-in, ever.

The question we're proudest to answer
04

What does a project actually cost?

Most land between $6k and $18k depending on page count and how much copy we write versus adapt. You'll get a fixed quote before anything starts — no surprise hourly creep.

05

Can you plug into our developers?

Yes — we ship clean, semantic markup and annotated specs your team can build against. We'll join two standups a week if that keeps the handoff tight.

06

What if we need to pause halfway?

Life happens. Pause for up to 60 days at no charge and we hold your slot. Anything already delivered is yours to keep, whatever you decide next.

07

Do you offer ongoing support?

A monthly retainer covers tweaks, new sections, and quarterly performance reviews — optional, month-to-month, cancel with two weeks' notice.
