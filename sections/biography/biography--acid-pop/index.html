Building Loophole — the feedback tool that turns support noise into a ranked roadmap.

Priya spent nine years shipping developer tooling — first as engineer #4 at a monitoring startup, then leading the platform team through a rough, instructive acquisition. She started Loophole after watching three good products die from guessing what users wanted instead of reading what they already said.

These days she writes most of the changelog herself, still reviews every onboarding email, and keeps a running list of features she has deliberately refused to build. She believes small teams win by being sharp, not by being loud.
