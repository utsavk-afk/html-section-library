Marème Diallo

Founder & CEO, Rowan Analytics

I build tools for the people who read the dashboards nobody else opens — the ops leads, the on-call engineers, the finance teams closing the books at 11pm.

Before Rowan, I spent six years on data platform teams at Wise and a mid-stage logistics startup, where I learned that most reporting tools fail in the same quiet way: they answer the question you already knew to ask, and go silent on the one that would have saved you. I started Rowan in 2021 to close that gap — a monitoring layer that watches your metrics the way a good analyst would, and taps you on the shoulder before the number in the board deck goes red.

Today we're a remote team of fourteen across five time zones, profitable since our second year, and stubbornly bootstrapped. I still review every incident retro and answer support tickets on Fridays — it's the fastest way I know to stay honest about what we ship.
