Founder

Mara Okonkwo

Founder & Principal Engineer at Halyard Systems — previously infrastructure lead at Cloudflare and Stripe.

Mara started writing payment reconciliation code because a former employer kept losing three cents on every transaction and nobody could say why. Fifteen years later she runs a nine-person team that ships ledger infrastructure quietly trusted by fintechs in eleven countries.

Her work sits at the awkward seam between correctness and speed — the belief that a system can be both provably right and fast enough to feel invisible. She wrote the open-source Tallow double-entry engine, mentors two apprentices a year, and keeps a running list of every bug that ever cost a customer money, framed above her desk.

Outside the terminal she restores mechanical typewriters, coaches a junior chess club on Saturdays, and is slowly failing to learn the cello.
